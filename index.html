About Us

Welcome to our innovative journey with the smart boat "Vikrant." We are committed to revolutionizing water cleaning processes with state-of-the-art technology.

The smart boat, equipped with advanced IoT sensors, is designed to efficiently tackle floating waste and water hyacinth in lakes and water bodies. Our commitment lies in creating a sustainable and cleaner environment.

Join us in this mission as we strive to make a positive impact on aquatic ecosystems, providing intelligent solutions for a better, cleaner future. It's worth noting that our smart boat "Vikrant" is not only a technological marvel but also features patented technology, ensuring that our innovation stands out in the field.
